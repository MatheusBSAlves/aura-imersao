# Project Brief & PRD: Studio Aura Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
**Client:** Marina Vasconcelos, Studio Aura
**Industry:** High-end Interior Design & Architecture
**Objective:** Create an elegant, sophisticated landing page that functions as an "experience" rather than just a website, targeting a luxury clientele in São Paulo.
**Core Aesthetic:** "Quiet Luxury" — minimalist, dark tones, gold accents, boutique hotel/art gallery feel.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- High-net-worth individuals in São Paulo.
- Clients looking for bespoke, high-standard interior design.
- People who value exclusivity, attention to detail, and a refined aesthetic.

---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Key Sections
- **Hero / Opening:** Immersive background video (`interior-dark-video.mp4`) with a compelling headline.
- **Methodology (Nosso Processo):** A 3-step breakdown of the work process (Concept, Design, Creation). Includes a scroll-triggered video component (`video-camadas.mp4`).
- **Portfolio (Projetos Selecionados):** A curated gallery of 6+ high-resolution interior design projects.
- **About the Founder (Marina Vasconcelos):** Narrative section with founder bio and professional headshot.
- **Testimonials (Depoimentos):** Social proof from satisfied high-end clients.
- **Contact & Lead Gen:** Detailed contact form, direct WhatsApp inquiry link, and office location.
- **Footer:** Social media links (Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest) and legal copyright information.

### 3.2 Technical Requirements
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for Desktop and Mobile.
- **Media Assets:** Integration of MP4 video backgrounds and high-fidelity architectural photography.
- **Interactions:** Smooth transitions, scroll-triggered animations, and hover effects on portfolio items.
- **Design System:** Consistent use of CSS variables for colors, typography (Bodoni Moda), and spacing.

---

## 4. Visual Identity & Design System
**Name:** Studio Aura
**Primary Palette:** 
- Background: Deep Charcoal / Matte Black (#121212)
- Accents: Champagne Gold / Brass
- Text: Off-white and muted gold for readability and hierarchy.

**Typography:**
- Headlines: **Bodoni Moda** (Serif) - Elegant, high-contrast, timeless.
- Body: Modern Sans-serif - Clean, professional, legible.

---

## 5. Content Strategy
- **Tone of Voice:** Professional, confident, artistic, and understated.
- **Language:** Brazilian Portuguese (PT-BR).
- **Key Message:** "Designing high-standard interiors that capture the essence of your lifestyle."

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Visual Alignment:** 100% adherence to the "Quiet Luxury" brief.
- **Lead Generation:** Successful submission of the contact form and WhatsApp clicks.
- **User Engagement:** Average session duration (measuring the "experience" factor).
